# Create your own avatar

🦸🏼‍♂️ Wether you don’t want to show up on a document or you want to create [a funny digital version of yourself](https://alicekeeler.com/2018/01/01/make-learning-better-bitmoji/), you may want to create an avatar.

There is several way to do this. On your iPhone (rather than on your iPad because you need a Pro one), you can create a [Memoji](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT208986).

⬇️ Or you can you one those site down below.

- [Bitmoji](https://www.bitmoji.com/)
- [Avatar gratuit](https://www.avatar-gratuit.com/)
- [Avatar maker](https://avatarmaker.com/)
- [avataaars generator](https://getavataaars.com/)
- [Kartunix](https://www.kartunix.com/simpleac)


See https://learn.framevr.io/post/frame-beta-update-avatars-environments-and-more (and https://framevr.io/)
